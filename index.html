Welcome to Elijohnology

We are a Malawi-based company founded by John and Eliya, specializing in innovative electrical and electronic solutions. Our goal is to design, build, and maintain systems that make life easier, safer, and more efficient for individuals and businesses.

About Us

At Elijohnology Electrical and Electronics Innovation, we combine creativity and technical expertise to bring your electrical ideas to life. With strong backgrounds in electrical engineering and electronics, our team designs and installs reliable systems tailored to client needs.

We believe in the power of innovation to transform communities and industries.

Our Services

  • Design and assembly of electronic devices
  • Automation and control systems
  • Renewable energy (solar system design)
  • Troubleshooting and repair of electrical systems
  • Installing and maintaining pumps (submersible and surface)
